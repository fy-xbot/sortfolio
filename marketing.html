用滑动卡片的方式，快速决定每张照片的去留。所有处理在本机完成，不上传任何照片。

iPhone · iOS 15.5 及以上 · 中文 / English / 日本語 / 한국어 / Español

核心功能

围绕「快速决定 → 安全归档」一条主线，所有处理离线完成。

↔

卡片手势整理

左滑删除、右滑保留、上滑收藏。专注当下这一张，不再纠结整本相册。

⊟

相似 / 模糊 / 截图

设备端识别近似照片、模糊照片与截图，按组浏览后再决定保留哪张。

▣

大文件清理

把占用空间最多的视频与长图集中起来，看到再决定，不必翻遍相册。

↧

图片 / 视频压缩

批量压缩占用空间大的素材以释放云端空间。属于有损压缩，过程不可逆。

◷

相册洞察

年度与月度回顾卡片，看清哪些月份照片最多、最常出现的人与地点。

✦

精选记忆

旧照片以浮现的方式回到首页，像 feed 一样自然刷到，重新发现被遗忘的瞬间。

隐私优先

归册不收集你的个人信息，照片永远不会离开你的设备。

  • 本地处理
    相似检测、美学评分、压缩等全部在设备端完成，不向服务器传送任何照片或视频。
  • 无第三方追踪
    不集成 Firebase、AdMob、Meta 等任何第三方分析或广告 SDK。
  • 不收集个人信息
    不要求注册账号、不读取通讯录、不收集精确位置。
  • 仅必要联网
    仅 Pro 激活、版本检查与远程配置会联网，全部使用 HTTPS。
  • 软删除
    「删除」操作把照片移入 iOS「最近删除」相簿，30 天内可在系统侧恢复。
  • 开放法律文档
    完整 隐私政策 与 用户协议 公开可查。

iPhone · iOS 15.5+ · English / 中文 / 日本語 / 한국어 / Español

Core Features

Built around "decide fast → archive safely". Everything runs offline on your device.

↔

Card-based gestures

Swipe left to delete, right to keep, up to favorite. Focus on the photo in front of you, not the whole album.

⊟

Similar / blurry / screenshots

On-device detection groups near-duplicates, blurry shots and screenshots so you can review and pick the best one.

▣

Large file cleanup

Surface the videos and long screenshots that take up the most space, so you don't have to dig through every album.

↧

Photo & video compression

Batch compress space-hogging media to free up cloud storage. Compression is lossy and not reversible.

◷

Album insights

Yearly and monthly recap cards reveal which months produced the most photos and the people / places that show up most.

✦

Featured memories

Old photos resurface on the home screen feed-style, helping you rediscover moments you'd forgotten.

Privacy First

SortFolio doesn't collect personal info. Your photos never leave your device.

  • On-device only
    Similarity detection, aesthetic scoring and compression all run locally. No photos or videos are sent to any server.
  • No third-party tracking
    No Firebase, AdMob, Meta or any third-party analytics / advertising SDKs.
  • No personal data
    No account required. We don't read your contacts and don't collect precise location.
  • Minimal network use
    Network is used only for Pro activation, update checks and remote config — all over HTTPS.
  • Soft deletion
    "Delete" moves photos to iOS's "Recently Deleted" album, recoverable for 30 days.
  • Open legal docs
    Read the full Privacy Policy and User Agreement.
