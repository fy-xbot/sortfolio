常见问题（FAQ）

归册会上传我的照片到云端吗？

不会。所有照片浏览、相似检测、压缩处理都在你的设备本地完成，不会上传任何照片或视频到我们的服务器。详见 隐私政策。

我滑动「删除」的照片去哪了？还能恢复吗？

归册不会立即永久删除照片。被删除的照片会移入 iOS 系统相册的「最近删除」相簿，保留 30 天。在此期间你可以从「最近删除」中恢复，超过 30 天后由 iOS 自动清空。

每日免费整理张数用完了怎么办？

免费用户每天可以整理 50 张照片。配额会在每日 0 点自动重置。如果想取消每日配额，可以在「设置 → 升级 Pro」中订阅或购买 Pro。

我拿到一个促销码，如何兑换？

归册的促销码通过 Apple 的标准兑换通道发放（即 App Store Connect 后台生成的 Promo Code / Offer Code）。在 iPhone 上有两种兑换方式：

方式一：在归册内

  1. 打开归册 →「设置」→ 点击「升级 Pro」打开付费墙
  2. 滚动到付费墙底部，点击「兑换促销码」
  3. 在弹出的 Apple 兑换面板中输入促销码并确认

方式二：在 App Store

  1. 打开 App Store → 右上角头像
  2. 选择「兑换充值卡或代码」
  3. 输入促销码并确认

兑换成功后回到归册，Pro 状态会自动同步。如长时间未生效，请在付费墙底部点击「恢复购买」。如提示「无效」请检查空格、字母大小写，或邮件联系我们核实。

我换了新 iPhone，Pro 状态会丢失吗？

不会丢失。在新设备上登录同一个 Apple ID → 打开归册 → 进入付费墙 → 点击「恢复购买」即可。无论你是直接订阅、一次性买断还是通过 Apple 促销码激活，Pro 权益都绑定在 Apple ID 上，由 App Store 自动恢复。如长时间未恢复，请邮件联系我们。

如何取消订阅？

归册的订阅由 Apple 管理。请前往：
iPhone「设置」→ 顶部你的姓名 → 「订阅」→ 选择 SortFolio → 「取消订阅」。
取消后当期仍可继续使用 Pro 功能直到到期。

「最近删除」里清空了，归册的应用数据会受影响吗？

不会。归册只读取 iOS 系统相册的状态，自身不存储照片副本。你在系统相册侧的任何操作（清空、恢复）都会被归册自动同步识别。

跨设备数据会同步吗？

目前归册的「已归档记录」「美学评分缓存」等数据存储在本设备本地，不跨设备同步。系统相册本身的同步由 iCloud 照片提供（如你已开启）。

相似照片 / 模糊 / 截图识别准确吗？

识别基于设备端的本地算法：相似检测使用感知哈希 + 时间窗口；美学评分使用本地化的 NIMA 模型。算法会持续优化，但仍可能存在误判，建议在删除前确认。

压缩功能会损失画质吗？

会。归册的图片与视频压缩属于有损压缩，目的是显著降低占用空间，过程不可逆。压缩前请确认重要素材已备份至 iCloud 或其他位置。我们在压缩页面也会做相应提示。

App 闪退或卡住怎么办？
  1. 尝试上滑关闭归册再重新打开
  2. 重启 iPhone
  3. 检查 App Store 是否有归册的新版本
  4. 仍无法解决，请通过邮件附上设备型号、iOS 版本、归册版本号联系我们
如何评价或反馈？

欢迎在 App Store 留下评分与评论 — 这对独立开发者非常重要。如果有具体的功能建议或 Bug 报告，邮件是更可靠的渠道。

可以删除我的相关数据吗？

归册不会收集你的个人信息。仅 Pro 激活会用到匿名设备标识符。如需删除服务器上与你相关的激活记录，请邮件联系，我们会在 30 天内处理。详见 隐私政策。

Frequently Asked Questions

Does SortFolio upload my photos to the cloud?

No. All browsing, similarity detection and compression happen on your device. Nothing is uploaded to our servers. See the Privacy Policy.

Where do photos go when I swipe "Delete"? Can I recover them?

SortFolio does not permanently delete photos immediately. They are moved to iOS's "Recently Deleted" album, where iOS keeps them for 30 days. You can recover them from there during this period; after 30 days iOS automatically purges them.

I ran out of free daily organize quota. What now?

Free users can organize up to 50 photos per day. The quota resets daily at midnight. To remove the quota, subscribe to or purchase Pro from Settings → Upgrade Pro.

I got a promo code. How do I redeem it?

SortFolio promo codes are issued through Apple's standard channel (Promo Codes / Offer Codes generated in App Store Connect). On iPhone there are two ways to redeem:

Option 1: Inside SortFolio

  1. Open SortFolio → Settings → tap "Upgrade Pro" to open the paywall
  2. Scroll to the bottom of the paywall and tap "Redeem Promo Code"
  3. Enter the code in the Apple redemption sheet that appears and confirm

Option 2: In the App Store

  1. Open the App Store → tap your avatar in the top-right corner
  2. Choose "Redeem Gift Card or Code"
  3. Enter the code and confirm

Once redeemed, return to SortFolio and your Pro status will sync automatically. If it doesn't take effect, tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom of the paywall. If you see "Invalid code", check for stray spaces or letter case, or email us to verify.

I switched to a new iPhone. Will I lose Pro?

No. Sign in with the same Apple ID on the new device → open SortFolio → open the paywall → tap "Restore Purchases". Whether you subscribed, bought the lifetime tier, or activated via an Apple promo code, your entitlement is tied to your Apple ID and is restored by the App Store automatically. Email us if it doesn't restore.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. Go to:
iPhone Settings → your name at the top → Subscriptions → SortFolio → Cancel Subscription.
You'll keep Pro access until the current period ends.

If I empty "Recently Deleted", will SortFolio's data be affected?

No. SortFolio only reads from the iOS Photos library and does not keep its own copies. Any change you make on the system side (empty / restore) is automatically reflected.

Does data sync across my devices?

Currently, SortFolio's "organized records" and "aesthetic score cache" are stored locally on each device and do not sync across devices. The Photos library itself syncs via iCloud Photos if you have it enabled.

How accurate is the similar / blurry / screenshot detection?

Detection runs entirely on-device: similarity uses perceptual hashing plus a time window; aesthetic scoring uses a local NIMA model. We keep improving accuracy, but mistakes can still happen — please review before deleting.

Does compression reduce quality?

Yes. SortFolio's photo and video compression is lossy by design — the goal is significant size reduction, and the process is not reversible. Please make sure originals are backed up to iCloud or elsewhere before compressing. The app shows reminders on the compression screen.

The app crashes or freezes. What can I do?
  1. Swipe the app away to close it, then relaunch
  2. Restart your iPhone
  3. Check the App Store for an update
  4. If the issue persists, email us with your device model, iOS version and SortFolio version
How do I leave a review or feedback?

App Store reviews are very helpful for an independent developer. For specific feature requests or bug reports, email is the most reliable channel.

Can I delete my data?

SortFolio does not collect personal information. Pro activation only uses an anonymous device identifier. To request deletion of any activation records on our server, email us — we'll process within 30 days. See the Privacy Policy.
